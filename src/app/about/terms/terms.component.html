Collected Information:

  • If you choose to verify your account, I may require you to provide personally identifiable information. During authenticating with the ID provider of your country, the subject (claim "sub") of the OpenID Connect ID Token will be stored in a separate database table without date information and no link to your account. This information is stored to avoid multiple verifications with the same, unique subject, and to add some significance to the contributions of verified accounts. Combined with the hopefully secure database of the ID provider of your country, the stored subject might be used to reconstruct your real identity and that you are registered at serenditree.io, but your account and contributions stay pseudonymous. Be aware that pseudonymous is not the same as anonymous.
  • After registration and even before verification, every content you enter and any option you select might get enriched by temporal and spatial information and will be stored in a database upon your active request only. You can delete it or request deletion of any information related to your account and the account itself at any time. Spatial and temporal information is needed to port the natural dynamics of spontaneous order to virtual domains.

No Cookies:

Serenditree.io does not store any cookies on your device and does not employ any tracking technologies. It will be removed if you actively sign out or clear your browsers local storage.
